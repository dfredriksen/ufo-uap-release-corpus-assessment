## Initial Media Takeaway

The videos are a mixed media pool, not a clean set of UAP clips. The first visual pass shows at least three buckets:

1. Potential UAP/range-fouler media: small bright/dark points, tracked returns, over-water IR scenes, and sensor overlays.
2. Operational context media: boats, coastal ISR, urban/compound ISR, strikes, terrain, and offshore infrastructure.
3. Non-UAP or administrative media: NASA-logo video and likely contextual material.

The next useful step is to pull higher-resolution frame sequences from the high-priority videos and compare visible overlays, scene geography, and object behavior against the event timeline.
