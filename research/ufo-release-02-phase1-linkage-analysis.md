# WAR.GOV Release 02 Phase 1 Linkage Analysis

Owner: Dan Fredriksen
Created: 2026-05-25
Scope: Reconcile the Release 02 ODNI narrative bundle with its linked Release 01 evidence

## Scope

The official WAR.GOV Release 02 manifest shows one explicit Phase 2 bundle that points back to Phase 1 evidence:

- `ODNI-UAP-D001`, the senior USIC narrative

Its linked Phase 1 evidence set consists of:

- `USPER Statement about UAP Sighting`
- `FBI Photo A001` through `FBI Photo A008`
- `FBI Photo B001` through `FBI Photo B024`

That is `33` linked rows in the current inventory: one narrative statement and `32` still-image items. The bundle is a narrative-plus-still-image corroboration set, not a sensor/telemetry package.

## Reconciliation

### Narrative Alignment

The Release 02 ODNI narrative and the earlier USPER statement are strongly aligned at the level of story structure:

- helicopter mission over a sensitive test range
- FLIR, NVG, and naked-eye observation
- a hot object that split into two
- nearby orange orbs / glowing orb-like objects
- repeated appearances near or above aircraft in the area

That alignment supports continuity and corroboration. It does not, by itself, create a new physics claim.

### Still-Image Alignment

The FBI photo set widens the bundle into a still-image corroboration set, but the photos remain low-context single-frame records.

What the photo triage shows:

- the A-series is low-context monochrome reticle imagery with small objects near the reticle and no mission report
- the B-series has a little more context, but it remains redacted single-still imagery with no time-resolved sequence
- `B5` is the clearest control-like frame because no distinct central object is visible
- `B6` is the most structured non-control still
- `B7` is the strongest conventional-appearance comparator because the official description says it is consistent with a helicopter

The stills are useful for provenance and family-level corroboration. They do not independently validate the narrative's motion claims, split claim, chase claim, or object identity.

### Bundle-Level Conclusion

Taken together, the Release 02 narrative and the Phase 1 bundle support this narrow conclusion:

- the official releases are internally linked
- the narrative is corroborated by a related still-image package
- the bundle stays in the narrative/image lane
- the bundle does not rise to public kinematic proof

In other words, the Phase 1 material strengthens provenance and witness continuity more than it strengthens the physics case.

## Analysis Outcome

The correct treatment for publication is:

1. keep `ODNI-UAP-D001` as the strongest new Release 02 narrative lane
2. keep the linked Phase 1 statement and FBI photo set as corroborating evidence
3. do not promote the bundle into a kinematics or object-identification claim
4. keep the bundle as a source-request target for original imagery, timestamps, metadata, chain-of-custody, and any associated sensor package from the same exercise

## Follow-Up Requests

If the bundle is to be pushed any further, the next scientific request is for:

- original imagery and any video associated with the same exercise
- capture timestamps
- chain-of-custody information
- platform and sensor metadata
- any associated range or exercise documentation

Without that material, the linked bundle should remain a corroborated narrative-and-stills package, not a physics reconstruction case.
