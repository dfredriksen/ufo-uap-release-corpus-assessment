## PR34 Geometry Feasibility

Dedicated note: `research/ufo-pr34-d33-geometry-feasibility.md`

The PR34/D33 geometry pass tested the D33 `80 MPH` report value against the cleaned `4.0s-59.0s` manual-review track. The all-row cleaned track has net rate `8.455 px/s`, path-average rate `118.837 px/s`, and median step rate `84.039 px/s`.

If the path-average rate represented `80 MPH`, the implied slant range would be about `8.94 nm` at `2 deg` full-frame HFOV, `4.47 nm` at `4 deg`, and `2.23 nm` at `8 deg`. If the median step rate represented `80 MPH`, implied range would be about `12.64 nm`, `6.32 nm`, and `3.16 nm` for those same HFOV assumptions.

The public MP4 therefore does not falsify `80 MPH`, but it also cannot validate it. Actual FOV/zoom, slant range, platform motion, gimbal pointing, target coordinates, or telemetry would be required.
