## Required Caveats

The final report should repeat these limits wherever kinematics or origin claims are discussed:

- Public videos generally lack raw sensor metadata, FOV/zoom state, slant range, platform motion, gimbal pointing, and frame-level telemetry.
- Reported speeds, altitudes, shapes, and object descriptions are often report-derived rather than independently measured from public media.
- Image-plane motion is not real-world motion without geometry.
- Release-index labels can be wrong, incomplete, or inconsistent across DVIDS, War.gov, PDFs, and local filenames.
- Redactions prevent decisive identification or reconstruction in many of the strongest document-only cases.
- The historical archive/image subsets are not yet a strong basis for final scientific claims.
